Dean's Welcome Message

Well come to the Directorate of Research and Graduate Studies at Chinhoyi University of Technology. The Directorate of Research and Graduate Studies is responsible for the creation of an enabling environment to support and promote need driven applied and innovative research and graduate studies in the University. The Directorate promotes a close working relationship between students and their supervisors. It is our hope that this close interaction creates a conducive environment for advanced study and need-driven technology-based research to solve national issues on food and nutrition security, value addition and raw material beneficiation, industrialization, information and technology advancement and wealth creation through innovation and entrepreneurship. Our founding remit is to serve as the epicenter for graduate education at Chinhoyi University of Technology. We support Schools/Institutes in the design, delivery and administration of both coursework and research-based graduate programmes. With this view, we are cognizant of the fact that Departments/Centres under their Schools/Institutes will be content experts in their areas of jurisdiction. Our primary role is to complement and support academic units to instill research responsibility and effective supervision. We therefore, support graduate studies in ways that foster a culturally and intellectually challenging environment that will result in students producing high quality dissertations/theses, patentable and commercialisable research outputs.

Dean : Dwayne Johnson
