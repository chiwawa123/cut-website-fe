VOIP INSTRUCTIONS

  1. VoIP phone VoIP phone(SOFT PHONE, SIP PHONE AND BLUE ROOF ANALOGUE PHONES)

    2. Dial the number directly for example 4000 to 2130, dial 2130 from 4000.

  2. VoIP phone to Analog(MAIN CAMPUS EXTENSIONS NOT ON VOIP).

    3. Dial * followed by the extension number. For example 4000 calling 1224, dial *1224.

  3. VoIP phone to Switchboard

    4. Dial #0.

  4. Analogue phone to VoIP phone

    5. Dial *620, Wait for IVR(Recorded message), then dial the extension. For example 1224 calling 4000, dial *620, recording then 4000.
