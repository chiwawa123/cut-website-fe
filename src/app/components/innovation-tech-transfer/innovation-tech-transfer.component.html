The Academy of Teaching and Learning

Welcome to the Directorate of The Academy of Teaching and Learning

The Academy of Teaching and Learning is an autonomous unit which functions as the University resource centre for teaching and learning. The mandate of the Academy is to enhance the teaching and learning capabilities of lecturers and students at CUT. This is achieved through activities such as professional development workshops designed for academic staff that are aimed towards setting the parameters and guidelines for ensuring quality teaching and learning. In addition, the Academy of Teaching and Learning runs the Postgraduate Diploma in Higher Education, a One year diploma programme aimed at developing the requisite university instructional skills in lecturers. The Academy of Teaching and Learning is also actively involved in research that is focused on improving teaching and learning.
